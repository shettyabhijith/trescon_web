About

An exemplary board of highly accomplished advisors, and a senior management with over Eight decades of experience in the events industry is leading Trescon to create world-class exhibitions, some of the industry's biggest conferences and high-level summits - all over the world.

History

Since its inception, Trescon has successfully connected thousands of industry experts, technology providers, startups, investors, regional policymakers, government authorities to more than 20,000+ participants covering various industry sectors.

Services

With an ever-growing team of 250+ professionals in registered and partner offices across the world, Trescon delivers world-class B2B events covering emerging trends, future technologies, major industry sectors and economies - all with global standards and local know-how.
